Troubleshooting

Problem One: The Line Won’t Move

Problem: Turning the knobs does not move the drawing line on the screen. Solution: Make sure the knobs are turning smoothly and that the Etch-A-Sketch is held upright. If the knobs feel stuck, gently rotate them back and forth to loosen the internal stylus mechanism.

Problem Two: Lines Are Hard to Control

Problem: The line moves too quickly or it’s difficult to draw straight shapes. Solution: Turn the knobs slowly and carefully. Drawing on an Etch-A-Sketch requires practice, and making small adjustments to the knobs will help you create more controlled lines.

Problem Three: The Screen Won’t Fully Erase

Problem: After shaking the toy, faint lines from the previous drawing remain visible. Solution: Shake the Etch-A-Sketch more thoroughly in several directions to redistribute the aluminum powder evenly across the screen.

Problem Four: Knobs Feel Stiff or Hard to Turn

Problem: The knobs feel tight or difficult to rotate. Solution: Try gently rotating the knobs several times in both directions to loosen them. Avoid forcing them, as excessive pressure could damage the internal mechanism..
