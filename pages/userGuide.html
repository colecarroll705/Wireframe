How to Use an Etch-A-Sketch

Step One: Hold and Prepare the Etch-A-Sketch

Begin by holding the Etch-A-Sketch so the screen faces you and the two control knobs are at the bottom of the frame. Make sure the toy is upright so the internal drawing mechanism works properly. When the screen is blank, it is ready for you to begin drawing. The Etch-A-Sketch uses a mechanical stylus inside the screen, which creates lines as you turn the knobs.

Step Two: Controling the Drawing Line

Use the two knobs to control how the drawing line moves across the screen. The left knob moves the stylus horizontally from left to right, while the right knob moves the stylus vertically up and down. By turning the knobs individually or at the same time, you can create lines, shapes, and patterns. With careful control, you can draw simple designs or more detailed pictures.

Step Three: How to Erase and Create Again

When you want to start over, simply shake the Etch-A-Sketch gently. This redistributes the fine aluminum powder inside the screen, covering the lines that were drawn and clearing the display. Once the powder is evenly spread, the screen will return to a blank state so you can begin drawing a new picture.
